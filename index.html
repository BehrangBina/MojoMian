I’m Mojdeh

I am Mojdeh but I’ll go by Mojo.

I am an experienced graphic designer with 15 years of professional experience and a university degree in design. I specialize in logo design, branding, and creating clean, effective visual identities that communicate clearly.

One of my greatest strengths is understanding my clients’ vision. I listen carefully to their needs and create designs that align with their brand personality and goals. Whether a brand needs a playful, vibrant identity or a sophisticated, luxury aesthetic with a refined color palette, I can adapt my design approach accordingly.

While I always respect and follow my clients’ briefs, I also bring my years of experience and strategic design thinking to every project, helping businesses make the best visual decisions for their brand.
