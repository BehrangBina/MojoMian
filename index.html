I’m Mojdeh, but you can call me Mojo.
I’m a graphic designer with 15 years of professional experience and a university degree in Graphic Design. I specialise in logo design and branding, creating visual identities that are simple, thoughtful, and built to last.

One of the things I value most is understanding the people I work with. Every business has its own story, goals, and audience, so I take the time to listen before I start designing. Whether you’re looking for something bold and playful or clean, minimal, and premium, I shape each project around what feels right for your brand.

I believe good design is more than making something look beautiful. It should solve problems, build trust, and leave a lasting impression. I always respect the brief, while bringing the experience and perspective needed to help clients make confident design decisions.
